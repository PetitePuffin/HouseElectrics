About House Electric

Trusted electrical services with over 10 years of experience

House Electric is a government-licensed electrical contracting company delivering round-the-clock electrical repair, maintenance, and project execution services across Delhi and the NCR. With over a decade of hands-on experience, we have grown to become a trusted name in both residential and commercial sectors.

Our journey began over 10 years ago, providing basic electrician services in just a few localities of Delhi. Through dedication, reliable service, and strong word-of-mouth, we gradually expanded to serve the entire Delhi NCR region.
