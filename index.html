About Us

House Electric is a government-licensed electrical contracting company delivering round-the-clock electrical repair, maintenance, and project execution services across Delhi and the NCR. With over a decade of hands-on experience, we have grown to become a trusted name in both residential and commercial sectors—including schools, offices, government institutions, and industrial complexes.

Our journey began over 10 years ago, providing basic electrician services in just a few localities of Delhi. Through dedication, reliable service, and strong word-of-mouth, we gradually expanded to serve the entire Delhi NCR region. Our commitment to offering fast, 24/7 electrician services helped us reach and serve over 10,000 satisfied customers within our first decade.

Five years into our journey, we officially registered under the name House Electric to mark our evolution from a local electrician service to a professional contracting company. Today, we proudly hold a 5-star rating on Google with over 500 positive reviews, a testament to our consistent quality and customer-first approach.

Over time, we’ve expanded not just in reach but also in capability. House Electric now offers a comprehensive range of services including:

  • Electrical Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)
  • Emergency Repair & 24x7 Support Services
  • Air Conditioning Installation & Maintenance
  • Corporate and Institutional Electrical Solutions
  • Government Electrical Service Contracts

We have also partnered with leading MEP companies for the execution of large-scale projects, further strengthening our ability to manage complex and high-value installations with precision and efficiency.

At House Electric, we are driven by safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to delivering cost-effective, high-quality solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs.

Whether it's a quick repair, routine maintenance, or a large infrastructure project—House Electric delivers with integrity, skill, and speed.
